Taiwan Commercial Real Estate

Improved profits, cashflow and automated investments process

Taiwan Commercial Real Estate

This is the first AI initiated real estate deal in Asia.

There are large number of real estate listing every day in Taiwan, with various size, unit price, floor and layout across different districts. It has been an intensive efforts to manually look into each of these and perform due diligence for shortlisted ones and be quick enough to catch up with the market. Once the deal is proceeded, the target property has been revamped into a business centre which is more complicated. As an investment company, it is always a challenge to keep all investors up-to-dated regarding the deal status, on-going operational data and distribute profits effectively.

The CM2’s AI Investment Manager solved these problems with the CUBE AI and CM2 RPA. The AI Investment Manager searched thousands of listing data, analyse it and identified a commercial real estate located in Zongshan district, Taipei that was under market price. The AI initiates the buying decision and both the tenancy agreements and investment agreements were automated by robots. The rental income collected are matched with tenancy agreements and the profit are distributed by CM2’s pre-built bots – which the collections are greatly improved and investors are happy with the just-in-time distributions.

CM2 solutions uniquely integrated the entire process from deal sourcing, due diligence, contracting, collection and distribution of profits on an intelligent and robotic platform. Today, the CUBE AI and CM2 RPA are monitoring the value of this property, running operations and recommendations the next best actions everyday.

“This is an amazing experience. The transparency and the new way of doing investments is going to become the de-facto standard.” Dr. Chang, Chief Secretary of World Taiwanese Chambers of Commerce.

J&E International

Engage customers better with actionable insights

J&E International

J&E International is a real estate broker located in Hong Kong and Taiwan, specializing in high value purchases and rents for residential and commercial real estate. Since the majority of their customers are high net worth individuals, institutional or veteran investors, the business expansion is hindered by the limited resources that can effectively engage with customers by quickly putting together comprehensive reports on real estate valuations, economic and financial data and relevant due diligence data so that they can compete with the large brokers.

After adopting CUBE AI, J&E agents can provide comprehensive reports about any properties instantly to their customers. It saves them a lot of time and effort in tedious paper works, and now they can serve their customers better by deep engagement. More importantly, CUBE AI is able to generate valuation of the subject property with 3 years forecast based on the anticipated economic growth, demographic movement, mortgage rate and even stock market index. It offers them an unique competitive edge over the large brokers as never before.

With the AI deal sourcing capability as well as the tracking functions from CUBE AI, J&E agents can proactive reach out to their customers with hot deals, updated valuation and market insights. It has significantly improved the sales productivities, customer engagement and operation efficiency.

“As a mid-size broker firm, we always strive for customer personal touch. CUBE AI allows me to get rid of tons tedious works and focus back in customers to generate more revenue and growth.” Edward Liang, Owner of J&E International.

Veteran Property Investors

Investing like a bank with full due diligence ability

Veteran Property Investors

One of the major pitfall of individuals investors in real estate, no matter veteran or rookie, is the capacity to process large amount of data to identify nice deals in the market and the difficulty in putting together a decent due diligence report before making the decisions. Even for the case of Miss Chuang, who is retired from a major bank in Taiwan and was responsible for real estate underwriting before retirement, she find it extremely difficult to complete these by her own when she wants to invest in real estate – even though she has all the skillset and knowledge.

Miss Chuang is an early adopter of CM2’s AI Investment Manager. She uses AI Investment Manager to monitor and analyse real estate listings 24x7 for the valuations and price gaps. After identifying a potential high value deals, the AI Investment Manager put together comprehensive reports on the property, including valuation, 3 year predictions, price trends, buyer / seller market index, demographical, economic and financial data, zone affordability level trend, zone price-income ratio trend, properties segment analysis and many more, with the only objective to provide real estate investor actionable insights to close a better deal.

As an individual investor without large supporting resources, she achieves similar result as she was working for a bank. In addition, the AI recommendation can identify similar properties that beyond ordinary human judgement and the number of alternative has significantly increased, which eventually turns into competitive edge in deal negotiations.

“I have never imagined that I would be able to achieve similar deal sourcing and due diligence results as I were working for banks. I always know my negotiation position before even talk to the owner.” Miss Cherry Chuang, Retired banker.
