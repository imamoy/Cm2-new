OUR STORY
公司背景

WHO WE ARE

CM2 provides intelligent and robotic real estate investment solution for investors, brokers and financial institutions. We are a PropTech company setting the new standards for real estate investments by combining AI and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) technology. We build the most accurate property valuations in Asia, enable AI-aided investment decisions, manage property contracts and automate real estate investment processes as never before.

我們的使命

OUR MISSION

Allow you to move faster than your competitors.

Create a predictable performance for investments.

Set you free from repetitive tasks and focus on growth.

公司願景

OUR VISION

Delivering via Software-as-a-Service, we make real estate investments and its processes more predictable, more agile and much faster. We hope to improve the real estate ecosystem by creating a new, simple, and precise way to invest in real estate.

為何是CM2?

WHY CM2

CM2 stands for CM Square. We believe the traditional real estate can be sliced and diced into as granular as square centimetre for accurate predictions, and becomes more agile to manage and easy to invest.

Powered by AI and robotic technology, delivering on the cloud and accessible from mobile, our solution consists of an accurate valuation engine, AI investment manager, industry-specific robotic process automation solution and pre-built bots that connect everything you needed to invest and manage real estate in a better way.
